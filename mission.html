Overview

The project’s main events include the online photo competition “Children, Pure Blossoms” (August 2018), “Child Abuse Prevention” talk (September 2018) and “Charity Day” at a yet-to-be-specified orphanage.

Blosshine Project was founded in 2018 by Bui Dinh Tri (student of Hanoi - Amsterdam High School for the Gifted) with the help of Water Wise Vietnam Organization.

Mission

Through these events, Blosshine aims to:

+ Equip primary school students with means of self-defence methods against abuse.

+ Spreading the passage of “No More Child Abuse” to the community.

+ Make students feel safe at all times.
