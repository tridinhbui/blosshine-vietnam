Music Show

Inspired by the pure and fresh start of the morning, PRISTINE 19: AURORA & Blossing - the first official show of The Strings combined with Blosshine.

Blosshine always aims to prevent sexual violence and abuse against children as well as to bring happiness to children in difficult circumstances. Contributing to fulfilling this mission, all the profits from the show will be sent to the orphanage, compensating the disadvantages they suffer.

Aurora & Blossing - the light from dawn - is not only a light of tenderness, bringing pleasure after a dark, dark night, but also symbolizing the innocence and purity of the young soul. In this event, Come to Pristine 19: Aurora, you will experience the full range of love.

Teaching

The Blosshine Project has officially ended teaching activities at Pascal International School on child abuse and sexual abuse as well as the skills needed to prevent and deal with relevant situations.

The two lectures must have left a lot of emotions for not only the project members but also for all the 5th graders participating in the study. Blosshine would like to thank Pascal Primary School for creating the opportunity and conditions for the project, thank the students for their enthusiastic participation.

Charity Day 2018

Blosshine's Peach Blossom Tet event took place very meaningful with the activity of making Chung Cake and giving Tet gifts to the children at Hanoi Social Protection Center III.

Blosshine presented the center with more than 70 Chung Cake and amounted to $ 300. The gifts are small but full of love, sincerity and enthusiasm of the project members!

The organizers hope all the children and Blosshine members have had meaningful, warm and memorable moments!
